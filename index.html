Welcome to my website

Hi! I'm Peter, a software developer and game programmer based in Canada. My passion for game development began early, sparked by modding a childhood game, that introduced me to the world of programming. I’ve since continued to grow my skills through formal education, studying Game Programming at Humber College and Information Technology at York University. I enjoy participating in game jams, as they are instrumental in honing my skills in time management, project scoping, and team coordination within fast-paced development cycles. These experiences have also provided valuable opportunities to collaborate with others and continuously expand my technical and creative skill set. Here, you’ll find some of the projects I’ve worked on, each reflecting my dedication to creating engaging and meaningful interactive experiences.
